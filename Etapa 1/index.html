About BMASkates

BMASkates comes from the CEO-Founder of the shop, Bardita Mihai Andrei. It is an abbreviation. We here go by the quote When nothing is true, everything is permitted and we think that this summarizes what skating actually mean. Being true to your cause, to your passions and when there are lies, then there needs to be justice. The rebel soul takes action. Come to the shop and find out what your young soul's all about
